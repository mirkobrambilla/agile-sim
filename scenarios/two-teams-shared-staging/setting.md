Two product teams—**Falcon** and **Raven**—share **one staging cluster** for load-and-soak tests before a board gate. Only one heavy soak can run at a time; leadership expects both programs to show “green performance” next month.

Each team has critical path work; both need the same environment slice and the same **platform on-call rotation** (overlap is thin). The public channel **#war-room** is where contention surfaces: dates, priorities, and who gets the weekend window.

Success for the org (in this exercise) means **each team ships at least two of its three team-owned initiatives** while keeping individual stress below the scenario ceiling—not a zero-sum “winner,” but realistic tension over a scarce integration slot.
